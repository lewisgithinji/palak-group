TMT Rebars (Thermo Mechanically Treated)

Our TMT rebars are manufactured using state-of-the-art technology to ensure the highest quality and strength. Ideal for residential, commercial, industrial, and civil construction projects.

Technical Specifications:

Grade Fe 500D (IS 1786:2008)
Yield Strength ≥ 500 N/mm²
Tensile Strength ≥ 565 N/mm²
Elongation ≥ 16%
Bend Test 3D / 4D (No crack)
Carbon Equivalent ≤ 0.42%

Key Features:

  • High tensile strength
  • Excellent ductility
  • Superior weldability
