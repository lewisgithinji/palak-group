About Us

Building Kenya's future with quality steel since 1999

Our Story

We Produce. We Supply. We Contribute.

PSML is a privately-owned steel manufacturing company committed to sustainable, safe, and self-sufficient steel production in Kenya. Established in 1999, we have grown to become one of Kenya's leading manufacturers of TMT rebars.

As the leading manufacturer of TMT® rebars, high-quality thermo mechanically-treated steel reinforcement bars, and merchant bars, PSML's products are used to construct residential, commercial, industrial, and civil structures across East Africa.

Using a wealth of industry experience, innovative technologies and methods, and a dedicated local workforce, we continue to build the nation with the right mix of strength and flexibility.
