About Us

We Deliver The Most Advanced Steel For Your Construction

PSML is a privately-owned steel manufacturing company committed to sustainable, safe, and self-sufficient steel production in Kenya.

As the leading manufacturer of TMT® rebars, high-quality thermo mechanically-treated steel reinforcement bars, and merchant bars, PSML's products are used to construct residential, commercial, industrial, and civil structures.

Using a wealth of industry experience, innovative technologies and methods, and a local workforce, we continue to build the nation with the right mix of strength and flexibility.
