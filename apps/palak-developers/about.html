Our Story

Building Dreams Into Reality

Palak Developers was founded in 1999 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create exceptional living and working spaces that enhance the quality of life for Kenyans. As part of The Palak Group, we bring decades of construction expertise and a commitment to excellence.

Over the past 25 years, we've grown from a small construction firm to one of Kenya's most trusted developers. Our portfolio spans residential apartments, commercial complexes, mixed-use developments, and infrastructure projects across Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and beyond.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Every project we undertake reflects our dedication to craftsmanship and our belief that great spaces create great communities.
