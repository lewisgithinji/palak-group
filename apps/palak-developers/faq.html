General Questions

What types of projects does Palak Developers handle?

+

Palak Developers specializes in a wide range of construction projects including residential buildings (houses, apartments, villas), commercial properties (office buildings, retail spaces, warehouses), mixed-use developments, renovations, and general contracting services. We handle projects of various scales from small renovations to large-scale commercial developments.

How long has Palak Developers been in business?

+

Palak Developers has been serving Kenya for over 25 years since our establishment in 1999. We are part of The Palak Group, a trusted name in East African business. Our decades of experience have allowed us to complete hundreds of successful projects and build lasting relationships with our clients.

What areas do you serve?

+

While our head office is located in Westlands, Nairobi, we serve clients throughout Kenya. We have successfully completed projects in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and other major cities. For regional projects, we assess each opportunity individually to ensure we can deliver our standard of excellence.

Do you work with individual homeowners or only corporate clients?

+

We work with both individual homeowners and corporate clients. Whether you're building your dream home or developing a commercial complex, we bring the same level of professionalism, quality, and attention to detail to every project regardless of size.

Services & Process

What is your construction process from start to finish?

+

Our process includes: 1) Initial Consultation & Site Visit, 2) Design & Planning Phase, 3) Budgeting & Contract Signing, 4) Permit Acquisition, 5) Construction Phase with regular progress updates, 6) Quality Inspections, 7) Project Handover, and 8) Post-Construction Support. We keep you informed at every stage and ensure transparency throughout.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

+

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and approvals from the relevant authorities including the National Construction Authority (NCA), county governments, and NEMA environmental clearances. This is included in our comprehensive project management service, saving you time and ensuring full compliance with Kenyan building regulations.

Can I customize my project design?

+

Absolutely! We encourage client input and customization. Our architecture and design team works closely with you to understand your vision, preferences, and requirements. We provide multiple design options and revisions to ensure the final plan perfectly matches your needs while adhering to structural integrity and building codes.

Do you provide architecture and design services?

+

Yes, we offer complete architecture and design services as part of our comprehensive construction solutions. Our in-house team of qualified architects can create custom designs or work with plans you've already developed. We use modern design software including BIM for accurate 3D visualization.

Pricing & Payment

How do you calculate project costs?

+

Project costs are calculated based on several factors: project size and scope, materials specification, design complexity, site conditions, and timeline requirements. After the initial consultation, we provide a detailed quote breaking down all costs including materials, labor, permits, and contingencies. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept bank transfers, cheques, and mobile money payments (M-Pesa) for your convenience. Payment is typically structured in milestones tied to project progress: initial deposit, foundation completion, roofing, finishing, and final handover. This protects both parties and ensures work quality at each stage.

Do you offer financing options?

+

While we don't directly offer financing, we have partnerships with several Kenyan banks and financial institutions that provide construction loans and mortgages. We can introduce you to these partners and assist with the documentation required for loan applications. Many of our clients have successfully secured financing through these channels.

Can I get a free quote?

+

Yes, we provide free initial consultations and preliminary cost estimates. After an initial discussion about your project, we can visit the site and provide a ballpark figure. For a detailed, accurate quote, we'll need to conduct a thorough site assessment and review your design requirements, which is also provided at no cost.

Timeline & Delivery

How long does a typical project take?

+

Timeline varies by project size and complexity. A typical residential house takes 6-12 months, while larger commercial projects may take 12-24 months. Renovations can be completed in 2-6 months depending on scope. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase and commit to realistic deadlines with regular progress updates.

What happens if there are delays?

+

We plan projects with buffer time to account for unforeseen circumstances like weather or material delivery delays. If delays occur, we immediately communicate with you, explain the cause, and provide a revised timeline. Our contracts include clear terms regarding delays and any associated costs or compensations.

Can I track my project progress?

+

Yes! We provide regular progress reports with photos and updates. You'll have a dedicated project manager who is your main point of contact. We encourage site visits at agreed intervals so you can see the progress firsthand. We also provide milestone completion certificates before proceeding to the next payment stage.

After-Sales Support

Do your projects come with a warranty?

+

Yes, all our projects come with a comprehensive warranty. We typically provide a 12-month defects liability period where we'll address any issues arising from workmanship or materials at no extra cost. Structural components often carry extended warranties. Specific warranty terms are detailed in your contract.

What maintenance services do you offer?

+

We offer ongoing maintenance services including regular inspections, repairs, renovations, painting, roofing maintenance, plumbing, and electrical work. Many of our clients opt for annual maintenance contracts to keep their properties in optimal condition. Contact us for a customized maintenance plan.

How do I report issues after project completion?

+

Simply contact us via phone (0791 240 000), email (info@palakdevelopers.ke), or through our website contact form. We prioritize warranty claims and aim to respond within 24 hours. Our maintenance team will assess the issue and schedule repairs promptly, often within 3-5 business days for non-emergency items.
