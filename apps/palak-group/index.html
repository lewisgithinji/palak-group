About Us

Building a Stronger Tomorrow

The Palak Group was founded with a vision to contribute meaningfully to East Africa's development. Over 25 years, we have grown from a single steel manufacturing unit to a diversified holding company with interests across multiple sectors.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and community development drives everything we do. Each of our companies operates with the same core values: excellence, integrity, and a dedication to building lasting value.
