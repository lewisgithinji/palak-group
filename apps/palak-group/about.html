Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

The Palak Group was founded in 1999 with a vision to contribute meaningfully to East Africa's development and growth. What started as a single steel manufacturing facility has grown into a diversified holding company with interests across multiple sectors.

Over 25 years, we have remained committed to our founding principles of quality, integrity, and innovation. Today, our four subsidiaries continue to build on this legacy, serving thousands of customers across Kenya and the region.

Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, strategic diversification, and an unwavering focus on excellence in everything we do.
